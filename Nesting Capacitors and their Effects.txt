1. The Simulation Engine
At the heart of the program is a numerical solver that simulates:
• 	Three capacitors in series (C1, C2, C3)
• 	A primary LC tank (Lp + Cp_inner + the combined outer caps)
• 	A secondary LC tank (Ls + Cs + load resistor)
• 	Mutual inductance between coils
• 	A voltage pulse driving the primary
It uses Runge–Kutta 4th order integration to evolve:
• 	Primary current
• 	Secondary current
• 	Primary capacitor voltage
• 	Secondary capacitor voltage
And it computes the energy stored in:
• 	Cp (primary capacitor)
• 	Cs (secondary capacitor)
• 	Lp (primary inductor)
• 	Ls (secondary inductor)
• 	Total system energy
This is the “brain” of the whole system.
