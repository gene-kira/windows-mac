Living Battery: Full Technical and Conceptual Overview

1. System Overview

The living battery is modeled as a multi-organ energy organism composed of two primary internal energy stores: a fast-response membrane layer and a deep-capacity core layer. These organs interact with environmental energy sources, internal metabolic processes, and an AI guardian that regulates bio-engine activity. The system integrates solar harvesting, ambient electromagnetic field capture, and a bio-inspired ion pump to maintain energy levels.

2. Energy Harvesting

2.1 Solar Harvesting

Solar power is harvested through a photoactive layer:

Formula: (P_{pv}(t) = \eta_{pv} \cdot P_{sun}(t))

2.2 Ambient Field Harvesting

Ambient electromagnetic fields contribute additional power:

Formula: (P_{air}(t) = \eta_{air} \cdot P_{field}(t))

2.3 Bio-Engine Harvesting

The bio-engine is an eel-inspired ion pump regulated by the AI guardian:

Formula: (P_{bio}(t) = \eta_{bio} \cdot u_{bio}(t) \cdot \left(1 - \frac{E_{mem}(t) + E_{core}(t)}{E_{mem,max}(t) + E_{core,max}(t)}\right))

2.4 Total Harvested Power

Formula: (P_{harvest}(t) = P_{pv}(t) + P_{air}(t) + P_{bio}(t))

3. Energy Distribution

Harvested power is distributed between the membrane and core:

Membrane: (P_{to,mem}(t) = 0.4 \cdot P_{harvest}(t))

Core: (P_{to,core}(t) = 0.6 \cdot P_{harvest}(t))

4. Internal Organ Energy Flow

4.1 Membrane to Core

Formula: (P_{mem \rightarrow core}(t) = k_{mem \rightarrow core} \cdot E_{mem}(t))

4.2 Core to Membrane

Formula: (P_{core \rightarrow mem}(t) = k_{core \rightarrow mem} \cdot E_{core}(t))

5. Load Handling

5.1 Membrane Load Supply

Formula: (P_{mem \rightarrow load}(t) = \min(P_{load}(t), \frac{E_{mem}(t)}{\Delta t}))

5.2 Core Load Supply

Formula: (P_{core \rightarrow load}(t) = \min(P_{load}(t) - P_{mem \rightarrow load}(t), \frac{E_{core}(t)}{\Delta t}))

5.3 Unmet Load

Formula: (P_{unmet}(t) = P_{load}(t) - P_{mem \rightarrow load}(t) - P_{core \rightarrow load}(t))

6. Losses

6.1 Membrane Losses

Formula: (P_{loss,mem}(t) = k_{loss,mem} \cdot E_{mem}(t))

6.2 Core Losses

Formula: (P_{loss,core}(t) = k_{loss,core} \cdot E_{core}(t))

7. Energy Update Equations

7.1 Membrane Energy Update

Formula: [ \frac{dE_{mem}}{dt} = P_{to,mem} + P_{core \rightarrow mem} - P_{mem \rightarrow core} - P_{mem \rightarrow load} - P_{loss,mem} ]

7.2 Core Energy Update

Formula: [ \frac{dE_{core}}{dt} = P_{to,core} + P_{mem \rightarrow core} - P_{core \rightarrow mem} - P_{core \rightarrow load} - P_{loss,core} ]

8. Temperature Model

Temperature rises with power flow and cools toward ambient:

Formula: [ \frac{dT}{dt} = k_{heat} \cdot |P_{flow,total}| - k_{cool} \cdot (T(t) - T_{ambient}) ]

Where:

(P_{flow,total} = |P_{harvest}| + |P_{mem \rightarrow load}| + |P_{core \rightarrow load}| + |P_{mem \rightarrow core}| + |P_{core \rightarrow mem}|)

9. Health Degradation

Health decreases from baseline aging, high temperature, and high power flow:

Formula: [ \frac{dH}{dt} = -\left(k_{base} + k_{temp} \cdot \max(0, T(t) - (T_{ambient} + 5)) + k_{flow} \cdot |P_{flow,total}|\right) ]

Health clamps to:

Formula: (0 \leq H(t) \leq 1)

Health reduces maximum capacities:

Membrane: (E_{mem,max}(t) = H(t) \cdot E_{mem,max,nominal})

Core: (E_{core,max}(t) = H(t) \cdot E_{core,max,nominal})

10. AI Guardian Bio-Engine Control

The AI guardian determines bio-engine activity:

Formula: [ u_{bio}(t) = \text{clip}\left((1 - \text{fullness}) + \text{bias} - \alpha \cdot \text{temp_excess} - \beta \cdot (1 - H(t))\right) ]

Where:

Fullness: (\frac{E_{mem} + E_{core}}{E_{mem,max} + E_{core,max}})

Temperature Excess: (\max(0, T - (T_{ambient} + 5)))

Bias: User-defined control input

(\alpha, \beta): Guardian weight parameters

11. Discrete Simulation Form

Each simulation step updates the system:

Membrane: (E_{mem}(t + \Delta t) = E_{mem}(t) + \frac{dE_{mem}}{dt} \Delta t)

Core: (E_{core}(t + \Delta t) = E_{core}(t) + \frac{dE_{core}}{dt} \Delta t)

Temperature: (T(t + \Delta t) = T(t) + \frac{dT}{dt} \Delta t)

Health: (H(t + \Delta t) = H(t) + \frac{dH}{dt} \Delta t)
